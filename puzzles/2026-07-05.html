How to Play

The abbey has commissioned the window on the left. Yours, on the right, starts dark. In your tray are fifteen shaped panes of red, yellow and blue glass.

Tap a pane to slot it into the window (tap again to lift it out). Every cell a pane covers takes on its colour — and where colours overlap, light mixes:

+=   +=   +=   ++=

Two panes of the same colour do not deepen it. Match the commission exactly in every cell to win.

Every flip — in or out — is counted. Par is 9: a true glasswright deduces the full answer before touching the tray. Beware — several panes fit the light almost everywhere…
