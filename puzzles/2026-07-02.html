How to play

Drag (or tap) from tile to tile to build one long chain. Each step must move to a touching tile whose number is exactly 1 higher or lower.

Equal numbers don't link. No tile twice. Start anywhere.

Any chain is a start — a perfect chain visits all 36 tiles, and every board has at least one. Drag backward to undo a step.
