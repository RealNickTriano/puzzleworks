How to Play

A single ray of lamplight enters the vault. Tap any brass mirror to flip its tilt — the ray bends wherever it strikes one.

mirror gem

Light all four gems at once to open the vault. Gems glow when the ray passes through them; they never block it.

Every flip counts. Match the par — the fewest flips possible — for a perfect ray. Reset any time to try again.
